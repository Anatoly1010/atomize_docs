EPR spectrosopy endstation is a multi-functional setup located at [the Novosibirsk Free Electron Laser Facility](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/7163372). The endstation comprises of 3 EPR machines, namely (i) a pulsed X-band EPR; (ii) a continuous wave X-band EPR; (iii) a time-resolved X-band EPR equipped with a Lotis TII Nd:YAG laser. Used hardware:
- **Pulsed machine**: Insys FM214x3GDA as multichannel 312.5 MHz TTL pulse generator, 2.5 GHz ADC, and 1.5 GHz DAC; BH15 magnetic field controller; Micran X-band microwave bridge v2; Lakeshore 335 temperature controller;
- **CW EPR machine**: Stanford Research SR-850 Lock-In Amplifier; Agilent 53131A Frequency Counter; Lakeshore 335 temperature controller; BH15 magnetic field controller;
- **TR EPR machine**: Keysight DSOX3034A oscilloscope; Keysight DSOX2012A oscilloscope; Agilent 53131A Frequency Counter; Lakeshore 335 temperature controller; BH15 magnetic field controller;
- **Other instruments**: NMR Gaussmeter Sibir 1;
